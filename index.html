Welcome

TO PROCIAL!



For Users -

  • Receive rewards based on your engagement with other users on the platform.
  • Get access to exclusive content of your favourite creators
  • Start or engage in communities that share your interest.
  



For Creators -

  • A place for content Creators and Influencers to create their community for multiple earning opportunities.
  • Earn reward tokens and advertising rewards for their content.
  • Rewards are directly proportional to the value of their content contribution.

For Companies -

  • Increase your brand value and sales by directly engaging with your target audience
  • Advertise opportunities on a decentralized network, and build transparency with the advertisers and brands.


For Fediverse -

  • Procial is a collection of interconnected servers that can be used for social networking, web publishing, and blogging.
  • Users can engage across other platforms in the fediverse. It's a perfect amalgamation of all social media into one.


Features


Feed :-

Post your work and thoughts to contribute to the community and earn throughout the process.

Networking :-

Network with a cohort of people with similar interests to create an effective following and earn PRX tokens while doing so.

Blogging :-

Write valuable content and create blogs to improve your profile's reach.

Chats :-

Chat with your network with end-to-end encryption and no surveillance.
